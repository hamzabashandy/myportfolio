A two storey house with a large pergola, the client wants to create more zones but the main goal was to create a 4 storey tower within he can enjoy the maximum view of the area surrounding him.
| Design Concept:
The design of the tower is inspired from the pigeons towers in Egypt. The lowest platform is linked by the house and have 2 access one from inside and one from outside. From this level you can take the stairs to a height of almost sixteen meters. The railings of the stairs and the platforms are made with wood.

B | THE FACADE

The facade is consisting of wooden louvers that are manually operated and were used to control the light and heat and to increase the indoor environmental quality. The choise of wood was as: it is a natural insulator, very strong material, renewable, biodegradable, recyclable, and they store carbon
