Oshtoora is a gathering of musicians, artists and anyone who is seeking alternatives. Itâ€™s a sponsor free celebration of a generation of dreamers. In 2015, it took place in a valley close to the coastal highway in Ras Sudr in South Sinai. In 2016, we moved to Al Fayoum, 100 kilometres southwest of Cairo next to Lake Moeris.

Our design team was responsible for designing a temporary village using local and environmentally friendly materials which could be constructed and removed without leaving any footprint, it should also be packed and stored to be reused in their next events. The structure was mainly wood covered with Henna Sheets (provide a shading solution with air ventilation ).

A | THE FIRST: 2015, RAS SUDR

The Village consists of 4 main Areas: 1. Concert zone. 2. The food court zone 3. Camp ground 4. Toilets and showers

The Tunnel

Within the glorious desert, we needed a significant the entrance, the beginning of our village. This inspired us for creating a tunnel. Also, it was used as a control area for administration.

The Food Court

The Food court area - was designed to accomodate the maximum numbers of visitors while providing shading at the same time.

The Stage

Many studies has been done to locate the best place for the stage. Two main factors has been taken into consideration : sound and wind. A sand hill was created to repel the wind. We used sand bags to protect the hill and at the same time a seat
