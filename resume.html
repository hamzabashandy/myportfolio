In 2015, I was a research assistant in Sustainable Building Design (SDB) Lab, University of Liège. I participated in a research project that evaluates energy performance of responsive facade technology in Belgium, giving me a chance to learn building and construction regulations in belgium in addition to interviewing prominent practitioners in the field such as Architect Philippe Samyn, AGC R&D centre, Colt, Seco and Daidalos Peutz.

Prior to that i was a part of the team at MADA Architects , one of Egypt’s prominent practices, where I was responsable of the visualization department, in addition to working on several projects where I assume full responsibility from the phase of proposed draft to construction and reception of the project. My experience also includes different projects in Egypt and United Arab Emirates.
